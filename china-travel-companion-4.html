Beijing

Capital · Best: Sep-Nov · 4-5 days

Beijing is China's capital with 3,000+ years of history. Excellent metro, English signs, and a mix of imperial grandeur and modern life.
Quick facts
Language
Mandarin
Currency
CNY ¥
Metro
Excellent
Budget/day
¥300-600
Mainstream Route
Forbidden City → Tiananmen → Temple of Heaven
Classic half-day walk. Start early.
Hidden Gems
798 Art District
Factory-turned art hub. Best weekday mornings.
Must-try dishes
Peking Duck 北京烤鸭
Crispy skin, pancakes, scallion, bean sauce
Iconic ¥120-200
🫕
Lamb Hotpot 铜锅涮肉
Copper pot, lamb, sesame sauce
Winter ¥80-120
🚇 Metro
27 lines. Yikatong card. ¥3-9. 5am-11pm. Bilingual signs.
🚕 Taxi / Didi
Use Didi app. Show destination in Chinese. Starting ¥13.
💳 Payment
WeChat Pay & Alipay. Foreign cards now supported.
🧧 Tipping
Not expected. 10% at upscale restaurants appreciated.
🍵 Tea Culture
Tap two fingers on table when someone pours tea.
📱 QR Codes
QR codes everywhere — payments, menus, tickets.
